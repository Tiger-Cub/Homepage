Xiaohu Chen

I am a PhD candidate in Mathematics at Capital Normal University (CNU), under the supervision of Professor Hongxing Chen (陈红星). Before this, I completed my undergraduate studies at Northeastern University at Qinhuangdao (NEUQ).

Research interests

I interested in t-structure on triangulated category and its application to the representation theory of finite-dimensional algebra. I also want to learn some topics related to DG category.

  • Triangulated categories with a compact silting object, Brown-Comenetz duality and Brown representability theorems (with Y.L. Sun and Y.H. Zhang) arXiv:2602.14383.
