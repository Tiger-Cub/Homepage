Research interests

Most of my work so far has been related to approximable triangulated categories. For details on this topic, one may refer to Professor A. Neeman's survey at ICM 2022, Finite approximations as a tool for studying triangulated categories.

Publications and Preprints

  • Properties of quasi-projective dimension over abelian categories (with H.X. Chen and M.G. Liu), J. Algebra 704 (2026), 139-164.
  • Finiteness of homological dimensions in triangulated categories (with H.X. Chen and J.B. Zhang), arXiv:2604.10478.
  • Triangulated categories with a compact silting object, Brown-Comenetz duality and Brown representability theorems (with Y.L. Sun and Y.H. Zhang), arXiv:2602.14383.
